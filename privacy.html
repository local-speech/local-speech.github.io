Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2025

🔒 AISpeech is an offline-first application. Your voice, files, and personal data never leave your computer.

1. Our Core Principle

AISpeech is designed with privacy as a fundamental requirement, not an afterthought. All speech recognition and file conversion processing happens entirely on your local machine. We have no servers that receive, store, or process your data.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Voice data — Your speech is processed locally by Chrome's built-in Web Speech API and is never sent to our servers.
  • Files — PDFs, images, and documents you convert never leave your device.
  • Typed text — Transcribed text is pasted directly into your active application via the local clipboard.
  • Usage analytics — We do not track which features you use, how often, or for how long.
  • Screen content — The app does not capture, read, or transmit any screen content.

3. Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored on your device only and is never transmitted:

  • Phone number — Used for device registration/activation. Stored in a local file (%APPDATA%/AISpeech/phone.dat). Never sent to any server.
  • Language preference — Your selected UI language (saved in browser localStorage).
  • Widget position — The equaliser widget remembers its screen position locally.

4. Third-Party Services

Google Web Speech API: AISpeech uses Chrome's built-in speech recognition engine. When speech recognition is active, audio may be processed by Google's servers as part of Chrome's Web Speech API. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. AISpeech itself does not send any data to Google — this is handled entirely by Chrome.

Paddle: Payment processing is handled by Paddle, our merchant of record. Paddle collects payment information (name, email, payment method) according to their own privacy policy. We receive only a confirmation of purchase and your email address.

5. Data Shared with Us

The only personal data we receive is your email address provided during purchase through Paddle. We use this solely for:

  • Delivering your license/download link.
  • Providing customer support if you contact us.

We do not sell, rent, or share your email with any third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

AISpeech is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Deletion

Since we store virtually no data, there is little to delete. To remove all local data, uninstall the app and delete the %APPDATA%/AISpeech folder. To request deletion of your purchase email from our records, contact us.

8. Changes

We may update this policy as needed. Material changes will be noted with an updated date at the top of this page.
