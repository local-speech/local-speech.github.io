Why AISpeech

AISpeech's local-first architecture eliminates the most critical risks users face with cloud-based alternatives.

🔒

Data Privacy & Security

Government agencies, banks, law firms, and hospitals are prohibited from sending internal data to external servers. AISpeech processes everything locally — your voice and files never leave your machine. All data is processed in memory and discarded. Zero leak risk.

📡

Works Without Internet

Cloud-based apps freeze when your connection drops or slows down. AISpeech runs entirely on your local machine — even license validation reads from a local file. 100% uptime regardless of network conditions, anywhere in the world.

⚡

Ultra-Low Latency

No network round-trip. No waiting for a server response. AISpeech polls input 20 times per second (~50ms) and pastes text at your cursor instantly via Ctrl+V. It feels like you're typing at full speed — true real-time dictation.

💰

One-Time Purchase, Lifetime License

Cloud apps charge monthly because they pay for servers. AISpeech uses your own CPU and RAM — no server costs. That means one purchase, lifetime ownership. No recurring fees, no surprise charges, no price hikes.

🖥️

Deep OS Integration

Web-based apps can't access system hotkeys or clipboard. AISpeech uses native Windows PowerShell and C# to integrate deeply with your OS — press F6 in any app (ERP, accounting, games, chat) and dictate. The lime equaliser floats on top, invisible until you need it.
